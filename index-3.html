About FBrands

We provide the best Design & Architects

FBRK (fa-brik) Brands, is a real estate and clean technology company. We exclusively focus on clean and green real estate development assets in the areas of mixed-use commercial, residential communities, and solar farms. Through the integration of renewable energy technology into the design of our real estate building envelope, we are doing our part to ensure the reduction of harmful carbon emissions that negatively impact our environment.

Current Project

THE TRECC EXPO

TECHNOLOGY

Fbrand's Workforce Certifications & Job Placement Expo is a continuous 3-month technology certification program that prepares youth, adults, and participants to earn at minimum $60k and up salary with benefits with a top ten fortune 500 company upon completion. Participants will learn basics of computer hardware, software, cyber security and emerging technologies.

RENEWABLE ENERGY

Our Renewable Energy Workforce Development Expo is a comprehensive program that exposes youth, adults, and participants for a career in the renewable industry field with a target focus on energy efficiency, air source, geothermal heat pumps, solar, wind, and batteries. Participants will have an up close in person experience learning from field experts and professionals.

CAREER READINESS

In the Career Readiness Expo participants will be introduced to high paying career fields. They will learn of the certifications needed to position themselves for these salaried focused careers with an attractive benefits package. This comprehensive program offers in-depth training and education in key sectors, ensuring a thorough understanding of industry standards and best practices.

CIVIC LEADERSHIP

Our Civic Engagement Expo, participants will learn critical principles to community building, foster creativity, and develop leadership skills. Participants will be exposed to the various levels of government in the U.S. and its impact on their day-to-day lives. They will be challenged to think critically about leadership concepts, traditions, and ways to improve their communities through active engagement.
